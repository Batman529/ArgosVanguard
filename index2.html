04 / Methodology
01

Ingest

Upload or connect your document repositories. Argos Vanguard parses .PDF, .CSV, .TXT, and .PPTX natively, extracting raw content, metadata, and structural context.

02

Index & Model

The engine builds a multi-dimensional index of entities, topics, relationships, and temporal markers. All data is modelled in a unified internal graph for cross-source querying.

03

Detect

Pattern algorithms scan for recurring structures, statistical outliers, entity co-occurrences, and hidden correlations that manual review would require thousands of analyst-hours to surface.

04

Report

Findings are delivered as structured, analyst-ready outputs with confidence scores, source references, and interactive visualisations — ready for decision support or further investigation.
